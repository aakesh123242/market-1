<strong>Electrical And Electronics Market Snapshot 2025-2033:</strong>

Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs) are increasingly moving product design and development processes to EMS partners. Product design is being outsourced to reduce overall costs and shift from fixed costs to variable costs. EMS companies are offering more design services for sub-assemblies and finished products. OEMS are collaborating with EMS partners and moving into new models such as joint design manufacturing (JDM) and outsourced design manufacturing (ODM). JDM model is further divided into high touch JDM and low touch JDM. High touch JDM is limited to DFx and valor analysis, whereas low touch JDM include board design and development. ODM model focuses on qualification and system integration testing, thus indicating a shift in electronic equipment manufacturing process.
